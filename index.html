Artistic

Overall the kind of art that most enjoy is traditional art. Sketching is the one that I practice the most since I find it easier to give depth to objects with simple black, grey, and white shades. Shown above is a W.I.P. of a rose that I have in my sketchbook. I've sketched most of my drawings with the same mechanical pencil for the last seven years, it's one of my best buddies.

Although sketching with graphite is what I usually practice, I have tried as of recently to start using color, specifically colored pencils. Carrying around a set of colored pencils can be more challenging that just carrying around 1 pencil, and because of this I usually don't do many colored drawings. Colored drawings tend to take longer to finish as well, which doesn't make them a good candidate when things are busy. As is clear in the image above, my flowers are my muse.

Collections

Recently I have begun to collect playing card decks. My brother was the one that got me into collecting playing cards after he got me a fancy deck of cards for my birthday once. What I look for in cards is that the design is intricate and it's definitely a bonus if the cards have a special finish like foil. Above are some of my favorites from my collection and it is clear that these are very detailed.

Nail polish is another thing I like to collect (*cough*...hoard...*cough*) even though I don't really use it that often. I have been told by many its an "unwise investment", but I don't see it as an investment; I see it as another artistic outlet. Above there are some of my collection boxes for my favorite nail polish brand. The brighter and shinier the better. I try my best to find the most fun colors and effects for this collection.

Good/Bad Habits

A hobby I have had to sadly put aside for the moment is reading. This "hobby" falls under the habit section because of my tendency to start a book and put it down roughly after 3 chapters, and then find another book I'm interested in and moving on to that one. This leads to handfuls of unfinished books on my shelf. But fun fact: a person needs to have at least 1,000 books to be considered to have a library, and I'm a 1/10 of the way there so I don't plan to stop reading.

And finally but certainly not least, is music. Even though I don't play any instruments I do listen to music a lot and it is something that I have considered essential since a very young age. My music taste has changed throughout the years, but I consider myself to have the same if not the same interest I have had in it since the beginning. Currently I listen to punk, rock, and pop music.
